## Get your data

1. Go to kalshi.com and sign in
2. Click your profile → Your activity
3. Click the Settlement filter
4. Click the download icon
5. Set date range: June 11 – July 19, 2026
6. Drop the CSV into the app

## Run locally

Download the repo and open index.html directly in your browser. No install needed.
